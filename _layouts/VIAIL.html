Our Mission

The VIAIL (Visual Intelligence and Analytical Imaging Lab), a hub of academic and experimental excellence, focuses on the intersection of computer vision, data visualization, and information processing (Visual Informatics) and AI/ML-based image processing and analysis (Analytical Imaging). Its academic research environment ensures a deep understanding of the theoretical aspects, while its experimental nature allows for practical application and innovation.

Lingustics

VIAIL (وئیل) in Pashto means “to saying” or implies "expressing knowledge", which metaphorically fits a research lab that “speaks through vision and images.”
